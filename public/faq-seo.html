Frequently Asked Questions - Agape Bible Church Bangalore

About Our Tamil Christian Church in Bangalore

Where is Agape Bible Church located in Bangalore?

Agape Bible Church is located at 12 B Cross, Sonnappa Block, Pillanna Garden 3rd Stage, Bangalore 560045, Karnataka, India. We are conveniently located and easily accessible from major areas of Bangalore.

What are your Sunday service timings?

We have two Sunday worship services: 9:00 AM and 11:00 AM. Both services feature Tamil preaching with English translation available for non-Tamil speakers.

Is this a Tamil church?

Yes, Agape Bible Church is primarily a Tamil Christian church serving the Tamil-speaking community in Bangalore. However, we warmly welcome people from all linguistic and cultural backgrounds.

How long has Agape Bible Church been in Bangalore?

We have been serving the Bangalore Christian community since August 5th, 1990. With over 30 years of ministry in Bangalore, we are one of the well-established Tamil churches in the city.

What denomination is Agape Bible Church?

Agape Bible Church is an independent, non-denominational, evangelical, and charismatic church. We focus on biblical teachings rather than denominational traditions.

Do you have programs for children and youth?

Yes! We have active children's ministry programs and youth fellowship meetings. Our Saturday youth fellowship meets at 6:00 PM, and we serve over 250 children across our churches in Bangalore.

What is your church's mission?

Our mission is "Reaching the unreached, Teaching the reached, and Touching the untouched with the Gospel of Jesus Christ." We actively serve Bangalore's communities, especially the marginalized.

Do you have parking facilities?

Yes, we have parking facilities available for our members and visitors. Bangalore traffic can be challenging, but our location in Pillanna Garden is accessible by both car and public transport.

Are visitors welcome?

Absolutely! We warmly welcome all visitors to Agape Bible Church. Whether you're new to Bangalore, exploring faith, or looking for a church home, you'll find a welcoming community with us.
